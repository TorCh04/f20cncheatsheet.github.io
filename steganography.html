1. What is Steganography?

  • Steganography: Science of hiding secret messages in non-secret media (images, audio, video, etc.).
  • Only someone aware of the method can find and decode the message.
  • Provides security through obscurity.
  • Messages can be hidden in images, audio, video, or other files without altering their appearance or function.

1.1 Steganography vs Cryptography

  • Steganography: Hides the existence of the message.
  • Cryptography: Obscures the content of the message.
  • Steganography + Cryptography: Encrypt message, then hide it.
  • Advantage: If no one knows a message exists, they won't try to decipher it.

1.2 Historical Steganography

  • Ancient Chinese: Notes in wax, swallowed for transport.
  • Ancient Greece: Message on shaved scalp, hair regrown to hide.
  • Wax tablets: Message on wood, covered with wax.
  • Cold War: Microdots (tiny photographs) embedded in documents.
  • Detection was rare due to tedious process.

1.3 Capacity and Security

  • Digital files used: images, audio, video, etc.
  • Capacity: How much info can be hidden in the carrier file.
  • Security: How well hidden the data is; resistance to detection (steganalysis).

2. Image Steganography

  • Most popular form of steganography.
  • Minor changes to images are not noticeable to the human eye.
  • Common method: Least Significant Bit (LSB) encoding.
  • Bitmap formats:
    • Color: 3 bytes (24 bits) per pixel (RGB)
    • B&W: 1 byte (8 bits) per pixel
  • LSB encoding: 1 bit of hidden data per byte; only ~50% of LSBs change.
  • PNG is ideal (lossless compression preserves hidden data).

3. Steganophony (Audio Steganography)

  • Hiding messages in sound files.
  • Methods: LSB, echo hiding, etc.
  • MP3 files (4-10 MB) provide many bytes for LSB manipulation.
  • Example: 10% of a 6 MB MP3 (~75,000 bytes) can hide a large document.

4. Video Steganography

  • Information hidden in video files (e.g., using LSB or DCT methods).
  • DCT (Discrete Cosine Transform): Alters frequency components in video frames (less noticeable).
  • LSB can also be used on video frame pixels.

5. Steganography Tools

QuickCrypto:
  • Hides text files only.
  • Large ratio of hidden data to carrier file makes detection harder.
Invisible Secrets:
  • Supports encryption and steganography.
  • Multiple carrier types.
OpenStego:
  • Easy-to-use tool for steganography.

6. Watermarking

  • Embedding identifying marks/text in files to prove copyright or ownership.
  • Example: Artists watermark digital art to identify their work.
